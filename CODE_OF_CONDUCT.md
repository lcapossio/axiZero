# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experience
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments in any form
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported by opening a GitHub issue or contacting the maintainers directly. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
